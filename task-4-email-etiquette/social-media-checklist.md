# Social Media Checklist for Students

## ✅ Do's
- Think before posting anything online  
- Use strong privacy settings  
- Share accurate and verified information  
- Maintain respectful communication  
- Build a positive and professional online presence  

---

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information  
- Avoid spreading fake news or rumors  
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or hate speech  
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown sources
