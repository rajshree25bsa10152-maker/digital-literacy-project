# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails  
- Always verify the authenticity of websites before entering personal details  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions  
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly  
- Do not share personal information on social media unnecessarily  

---

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes to receive money  
- Always double-check payment requests before approving  
- Avoid calls/messages claiming to be from bank officials asking for details  

---

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately:

- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- 📞 Helpline Number: 1930  

---

## 🎯 Purpose
This checklist is designed to help students stay aware and protect themselves from common cyber threats, especially financial frauds and online scams.
